About

Glamping U.P. was formed in December 2022 by two Medical Laboratory Scientists, Brooke and Robert, and one lab mix named Copper. After traveling the U.S. coast-to-coast working contracts at various hospitals, we chose Little Lake, MI as our place to call home, choosing it for its abundance of nature, clear night skies, and mountain biking trails in nearby Marquette, MI. We Currently own forty acres of wooded land in Little Lake, MI with access to bordering state land and beachfront on a small private lake, and plan to be fully operational by late spring 2024.
